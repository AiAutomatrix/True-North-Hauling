# **App Name**: True North Hauling

## Core Features:

- Header: Sticky header with business name, logo, and phone number (or clickable icon on mobile).
- Gallery Carousel: Interactive image carousel showcasing vehicles and services with navigation arrows.
- Service Listings: Display list of hauling, purchasing, and scrapping services with icons.
- Booking Form: Form to collect customer info, vehicle details, and preferred viewing time.
- Service Steps: Displays How It Works, What We Buy, and Service Area in card format.
- FAQ Accordion: Interactive accordion to answer frequently asked questions.
- Service Area Map: Embedded Google Maps iframe to show service area around Winnipeg.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, earthy green (#386641) to evoke trustworthiness and nature.
- Background color: Off-white (#F2F0EC) to give a clean, vintage feel, complementing the earthy green.
- Accent color: Muted gold (#A68A5A) to highlight calls to action, creating a sense of reliability.
- Font pairing: 'Belleza' for headlines (sans-serif, aligned to art and design) and 'Alegreya' for body text (serif, elegant and contemporary).
- Use solid, professional, recognizable icons, possibly with rounded corners for a modern feel. The style will be muted to complement the earthy tones.
- Single-column layout optimized for mobile responsiveness. Content sections are clearly divided to aid in easy navigation.
- Subtle fade-in animations for sections as the user scrolls.
